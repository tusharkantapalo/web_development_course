The best Movie According to Tushar

My top 3 movies of all time

Bahubali

Baahubali is an epic Indian film franchise directed by S. S. Rajamouli. Spanning two blockbuster films—Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) and Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017)—the story is set in the ancient, fictional kingdom of Mahishmati and follows a massive power struggle between two royal brothers, Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva.

KGF

KGF stands for Kolar Gold Fields, a historic mining region in Karnataka, India, and the title of a massive Indian action-film franchise. The location is a former boomtown that produced large amounts of gold for over a century, while the movies are blockbuster epics that tell the story of a daring mobster.

Venom

"Venom" can refer to the famous Marvel anti-hero or the toxic secretion produced by animals. Whether you are interested in the alien symbiote or biological stings and bites, this guide covers both.
